Parrot Island — Baratang

← Back

Overview

Parrot Island, near Baratang, is famous for the daily sunset roosting spectacle of thousands of parrots and parakeets. As dusk approaches, the birds circle the tiny mangrove island in waves before settling in for the night — an unforgettable natural show.

  • Location: Baratang, Middle Andaman
  • Experience: Short boat ride at sunset
  • Best season: Nov–Apr (clear skies)

The Experience

Boats depart close to sunset from Baratang jetty. As you approach the islet, the sky fills with flocks of parrots circling before landing in the mangroves. The spectacle lasts 20–30 minutes until the island is fully covered with birds.

  • Duration: 45–60 min round-trip boat ride
  • No landing — viewing only from boats
  • Best with binoculars or zoom lens

Visitor Info

  • Tickets: Buy at jetty/through local operator.
  • Departure: 4:30–5:00 PM (seasonal, confirm locally).
  • Carry: Mosquito repellent, water, camera.
  • Combine with: Limestone Caves or Mud Volcano during day trip.

Tips

  • Keep noise low — it helps the birds settle naturally.
  • Photography: Use higher ISO for low light; no flash.
  • Be flexible — sightings depend on season and bird movement.
