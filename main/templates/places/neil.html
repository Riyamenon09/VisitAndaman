Neil Island — Slow Island Life

Overview

Neil (Shaheed Dweep) is a compact, peaceful island known for shallow lagoons, natural rock bridges, and village charm. Perfect for cycling, snorkeling, and sunsets without the crowds of Havelock.

  • Ferry: ~1–1.5 hrs from Port Blair / 1 hr from Havelock
  • Ideal stay: 1–2 nights • Best time: Oct–Apr
  • Get around: Rent bicycle/scooter; roads are flat & scenic

Must-Visit

  • Laxmanpur Beach: Iconic sunset & Natural Bridge (locally Howrah Bridge) at low tide.
  • Bharatpur Beach: Snorkeling, glass-bottom boats; lagoon perfect for kids.
  • Sitapur Beach: Quiet sunrise spot with dramatic waves.

Visitor Info

  • ATMs & data are limited—carry some cash; UPI works at many shops.
  • Tide matters for Natural Bridge—aim for low tide; wear sturdy sandals.
  • Keep reefs safe: don’t stand on corals; avoid collecting shells.
